Featured Work
EasyDigz · PropTech SaaS · Triangle, NC · Ongoing

They had a product that worked. Marketing was starting from zero.

When EasyDigz brought me in, there was nothing to inherit. No content pipeline, no email campaigns, no video, no webinar program. The brand had no presence and the product — a white-label real estate platform for agents and brokers — needed real education to land with the right audience.

I started with a two-week audit, then built the entire marketing system around one core workflow. Plan the content, promote it through a multi-touch email sequence, run it live, record it, cut it into short-form clips, distribute across channels, and repeat. That one workflow powered everything simultaneously without adding workload.

The engagement has grown consistently since. What started as monthly activity scaled to twice-a-month major activations — webinars, product showcases and expo presentations. March 2026 became a 30-day content series with 30 videos produced in a single month.

Today EasyDigz has a 10,000 plus agent email list actively managed with multi-touch campaign sequences, 40 plus videos published, a fully operational content engine, and paid campaigns running on LinkedIn. I am still embedded with them and the scope keeps growing.
